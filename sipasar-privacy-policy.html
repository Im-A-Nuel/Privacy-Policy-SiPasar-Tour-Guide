Privacy Policy

SiPasar is a navigation and tour guide app for traditional markets in Yogyakarta. This policy explains what data we handle, why we handle it, and your choices.

Last updated 25 September 2025 UKDW & BAPEDA : Indonesia

Scope

This policy covers data processed by the SiPasar mobile app to provide routing, real-time navigation, simulation mode, and contextual tour guidance (text and optional audio).

Data We Handle

We collect only what is necessary to power navigation and keep the app reliable.

Location (precise or approximate)

  • Purpose: show maps, compute routes, and enable real-time navigation from/to markets and nearby POIs.
  • Collection: through OS location services when you grant permission.
  • Processing & Storage: used on device and in live requests to routing/map providers; we do not persist your real-time location on our servers.
  • Sharing: not sold; may be transmitted to processors strictly to deliver features (e.g., map tiles or routing APIs).
  • Control: you can allow, limit to “approximate”, or revoke location in device Settings. Simulation mode works without location.

Diagnostics and Crash Logs

  • Purpose: improve stability and performance.
  • Collection: when crashes or errors occur; may include device model, OS version, app version, and stack traces.
  • Provider: if enabled, we use trusted third-party crash reporting/analytics (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics) under their terms.
  • Retention: kept only as long as reasonably necessary for debugging and quality, then deleted or anonymized.

How We Use Data

  • Provide routing, real-time navigation, simulation, and tour guidance.
  • Monitor and improve app reliability, performance, and user experience.

Legal Basis

We process data based on your consent (e.g., granting location permission) and our legitimate interests in providing a functional, secure service.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with service providers strictly to operate the app (e.g., maps, routing, crash reporting). These providers are bound by agreements and process data on our behalf.

Retention

  • Real-time location: not stored on our servers; used transiently for navigation requests.
  • Crash/diagnostics: retained for a limited period necessary to resolve issues, then deleted or aggregated.

Children

SiPasar is intended for general audiences and is not designed to collect personal data from children under the age required by local law. If you are under that age, please use the app under parental guidance.

Security

  • Transport security (HTTPS) for network requests where applicable.
  • Least-privilege access controls for any internal tools and data.

Your Choices

  • Change or revoke app permissions in Settings > Apps > SiPasar > Permissions.
  • Use Simulation mode to try routes without granting location.
  • Uninstall the app to stop all data collection.

International Transfers

If we use third-party processors (e.g., crash reporting), your data may be processed in other countries with appropriate safeguards.
