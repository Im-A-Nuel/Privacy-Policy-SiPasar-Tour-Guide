Privacy Policy

SiPasar is a navigation and tour guide app focused on traditional markets in Yogyakarta. This policy explains what data we handle, why, and the choices you have.

Last updated 25 September 2025 Jurisdiction: Indonesia

Data We Handle

The app only collects the data it needs to power navigation and keep the experience reliable.

Location (precise or approximate)

  • Purpose: to show maps, compute routes, and enable real-time navigation.
  • Storage: processed on device; we do not store your real-time location on our servers.
  • Sharing: no selling of data; limited sharing only with providers required to deliver features (for example, map tiles or routing APIs).
  • Control: you can grant or revoke location permission in device settings. The app remains usable in simulation mode without location.

Diagnostics and Crash Logs

  • Purpose: to improve stability and performance.
  • Collection: only when you run the app; logs may include device model, OS version, app version, and stack traces.
  • Provider: if enabled, we work with trusted third-party crash reporting (for example, Firebase Crashlytics) under their terms.
  • Retention: retained only for the limited period necessary for debugging and quality.

How We Use Data

  • Provide core functionality such as routing, navigation, and tour guidance.
  • Monitor and improve app stability and performance.

Children

SiPasar is intended for general audiences. If you are under the age required by your local law, please use the app under parental guidance.

Security

  • Transport security (HTTPS) for network requests where applicable.
  • Access controls that follow a least-privilege approach for internal tools and data.

Your Choices

  • Change or revoke app permissions in Settings > Apps > SiPasar > Permissions.
  • Uninstall the app to stop all data collection.

International Transfers

If third-party processors are used (for example, crash reporting), data may be processed in other countries with appropriate safeguards.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to the app or legal requirements. We will update the "Last updated" date above and highlight significant changes in the app.
